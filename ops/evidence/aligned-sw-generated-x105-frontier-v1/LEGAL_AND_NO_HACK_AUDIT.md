# Legal and no-hack audit

- Private PIF searched/read/hashed/copied/staged/committed/packaged: no.
- Private ROM searched/read/hashed/copied/staged/committed/packaged: no.
- Firmware or commercial bootcode bytes/words embedded: no.
- Copied assembly, disassembly, or external source: no.
- Game/title/product-code/filename/digest/expected-trace selector: none.
- Hidden defaults, patch tables, instruction skipping, imported state, or
  replayed trace: none.
- Generic bus, generalized memory map, device registry, or generic store
  framework: none.
- PIF/IPL execution, authentic cartridge execution, BOOT-3, or compatibility
  claim: none.

All proof inputs are generated and all instruction words are encoded from
independent semantic fields.
