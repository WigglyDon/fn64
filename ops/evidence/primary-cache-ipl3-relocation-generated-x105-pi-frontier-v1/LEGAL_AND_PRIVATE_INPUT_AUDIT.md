# Legal and private-input audit

The proof uses only independently generated synthetic PIF-shaped bytes, the
repository's generated x105 fixture, and a synthetic Machine-owned cartridge
header word. Public N64-IPL source at pinned commit
928f59089c18a95cbffa59938a18fa6032c5d78c and public architecture manuals were
used for semantics.

No private PIF file, private or commercial ROM, proprietary firmware dump,
frontend, SDL window, RSP core, PI DMA, or authentic cartridge execution was
accessed or run. Small generated instruction words and public relocation bytes
are recorded only as needed for the bounded proof.
