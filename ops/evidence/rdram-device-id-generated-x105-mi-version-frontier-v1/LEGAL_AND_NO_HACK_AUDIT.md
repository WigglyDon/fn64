# Legal and no-hack audit

- generated PIF-shaped and cartridge bytes only;
- independently encoded public instruction fields only;
- pinned public definitions/operand relationships only;
- no private PIF or ROM searched, read, hashed, copied, executed, staged, committed, or packaged;
- no external source tree or authentic instruction stream copied;
- no trace bypass, identity policy, bus, MMIO registry, or address remap;
- no authentic execution, BOOT-3, or compatibility claim.
