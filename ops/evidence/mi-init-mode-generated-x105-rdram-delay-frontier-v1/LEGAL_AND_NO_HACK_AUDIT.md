# Legal and no-hack audit

- Private PIF firmware searched/read/hashed/copied/staged/committed: no.
- Private cartridge ROM searched/read/hashed/copied/staged/committed: no.
- Proprietary instruction stream copied: no.
- External source vendored or packaged: no.
- Title, filename, cartridge ID, digest, expected trace, or imported emulator
  state used as policy: no.
- Generated PIF-shaped bytes and generated cartridge bytes only: yes.
- Public source used only for definitions and bounded relationships: yes.
- Product proof passes through public `Machine::step`: yes.

Authentic checkpoint remains BOOT-2. BOOT-3 and game compatibility remain
unearned.
