# Legal and no-hack audit

- Private PIF or ROM searched, read, hashed, copied, executed, staged,
  committed, or packaged: no.
- External code, assembly, disassembly, firmware, instruction stream, or source
  tree copied: no.
- Generated inputs and independently encoded semantic fields only: yes.
- Title, cartridge ID, filename, digest, expected PC/value selector, imported
  state, replay, skip, patch table, or direct-entry staging: no.
- General CP0 bank, interrupt delivery, RI/MMIO, bus, map, or device registry:
  no.
- Authentic execution, BOOT-3, timing, or compatibility claim: no.
