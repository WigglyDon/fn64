# Validation

The candidate is validated with home-backed Cargo and fixture paths. Required
proof includes format, warnings-denied clippy, nonzero focused tests, the full
Rust forward gate, the public step probe, context/fleet/queue/link checks, a
fresh clean checkout, and post-integration canonical repetition. Exact command
logs, tested SHAs, final Context-SHA, and generated-path cleanup are sealed in
the Master artifact rather than committed as terminal-wall output.
