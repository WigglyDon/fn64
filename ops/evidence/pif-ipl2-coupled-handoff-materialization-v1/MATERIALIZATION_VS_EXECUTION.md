# Materialization versus execution

Named source-backed effects are sufficient for the bounded NTSC cold x105
entry: profile copy, cartridge copy, nine GPR source facts, Status, and the
completed control-transfer relation. Their sources and lifecycle are separable
without inventing intermediate PIF, SI, PI, or checksum behavior.

Minimal firmware execution is therefore not earned by this window. Execution
would introduce substantially broader devices and timing without being needed
to preserve the represented causal facts. PAL/MPAL uncertainty is handled by
fail-closed profile rejection, not by claiming execution is the only solution.
