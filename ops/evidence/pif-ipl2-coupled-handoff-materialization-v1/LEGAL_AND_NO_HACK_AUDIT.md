# Legal and no-hack audit

- Private PIF searched/read/hashed/copied/staged/committed/packaged: no.
- Private ROM searched/read/hashed/copied/staged/committed/packaged: no.
- Firmware bundled or downloaded: no.
- Firmware words, tables, assembly, or disassembly copied: none.
- External source vendored or compiled: no.
- Automatic search or hidden default: none.
- Game title, product code, filename, ROM digest, firmware digest, host region,
  or expected-trace selector: none.
- PIF/IPL execution, broad HLE, SI, PI, RI, DMA, interrupts, generic bus, or
  generalized memory map: none.
- Compatibility or BOOT-3 claim: none.

Only generated patterns and paraphrased source-qualified machine facts are
durable.
